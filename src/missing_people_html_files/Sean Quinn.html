Bio:

Sean Quinn graduated with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Applications from Dublin City University in 2017.

He received an Irish Research Council scholarship to undertake a PhD in Artificial Intelligence at the Insight Centre for Data Analytics, Dublin City University under the supervision of Dr. Alessandra Mileo.

Sean was a visiting research scholar at the Data Semantics Laboratory, Kansas State University for the fall semester 2019.

His research focuses on architecture‑agnostic neural transfer and knowledge‑enhanced neural transfer.

Research Interests:

  • Neural networks
  • Transfer learning
  • Representation learning
  • Knowledge representation
