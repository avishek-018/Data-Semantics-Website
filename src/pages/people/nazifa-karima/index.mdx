## Bio:

Nazifa Karima is a graduate student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Wright State University, working under Dr. Pascal Hitzler in the Data Semantics Lab.

She is involved with the GeoLink project, part of the NSF-funded EarthCube program that aims to implement semantic web technologies for improved data discovery, sharing, integration and representation.

LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/nazifakarima, GitHub: https://github.com/Nazifa115, Email: karima.2@wright.edu.
