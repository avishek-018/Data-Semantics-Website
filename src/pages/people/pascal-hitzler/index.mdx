# Pascal Hitzler

Pascal Hitzler is the endowed Lloyd T. Smith Creativity in Engineering Chair and Professor of Computer Science at Kansas State University, where he directs the Center for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (CAIDS) and the Data Semantics Lab.

## Research Interests
- Semantic Web
- Artificial Intelligence
- Neural-Symbolic Integration
- Knowledge Representation and Reasoning
- Machine Learning
- Denotational Semantics
- Set-Theoretic Topology

## Career Highlights
- Leads CAIDS and the Data Semantics Lab at Kansas State University.
- Former endowed NCR Distinguished Professor, Brage Golding Distinguished Professor of Research, and Director of Data Science at Wright State University.
- Earlier academic roles at the Institute for Applied Informatics and Formal Description Methods (AIFB) at the University of Karlsruhe and as a postdoctoral researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Institute at TU Dresden.

## Editorial and Community Roles
- Founding Editor-in-Chief of the *Semantic Web* journal and the IOS Press book series *Studies on the Semantic Web*.
- Co-author of the W3C Recommendation **OWL 2 Primer** and the book **Foundations of Semantic Web Technologies** (CRC Press, 2010).
- Serves on editorial boards and steering committees across neural-symbolic learning, ontology design, and related research communities.
