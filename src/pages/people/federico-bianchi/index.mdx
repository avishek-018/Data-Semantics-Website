## Bio:

Federico Bianchi is a PhD student at the University of Milano‑Bicocca who works mainly on knowledge graphs and knowledge graph embeddings.

He earned a master's degree in computer science in 2016 and started his PhD studies afterwards.

He is currently visiting the Data Semantics (DaSe) Lab at Wright State University where he works on neuro‑symbolic learning and reasoning systems.

Federico has served as a reviewer or subreviewer for conferences such as IEEE BigData 2018, ISWC 2017, AMI 2018 and AIIA 2018.

He can be contacted at federico dot bianchi [at] disco [dot] unimib [dot] it.

## Research Interests:


- Knowledge graphs

- Knowledge graph embeddings

- Neuro‑symbolic learning and reasoning
