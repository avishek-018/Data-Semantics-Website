## Bio:

Update: As of August 1, 2017, Adila Krisnadhi is no longer an active member of Data Semantics Lab. She is currently a faculty member at the Faculty of Computer Science, Universitas Indonesia.

She was a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Semantics Lab under Prof. Pascal Hitzler and received her PhD in Computer Science at Wright State University in December 2015, supported by Fulbright and research projects (TROn, OceanLink, GeoLink).

Her work includes research on extending ontology languages (nominal schema), semantic technologies for geoscience, and face recognition systems. She interned at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center in 2012.
