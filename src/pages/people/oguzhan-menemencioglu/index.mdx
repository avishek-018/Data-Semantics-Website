## Bio:

Oğuzhan Menemencioğlu received a BSc degree in Computer Education from Gazi University, Turkey in 2006 and an MSc degree in Computer Engineering from Karabük University, Turkey in 2011.

He is currently pursuing his PhD degree at Karabük University.

He has worked as a research assistant in the Department of Computer Engineering since 2009 and as an administrator at Karabük University’s Computer Center, focusing on distance education infrastructure, since 2010.

He is currently a visiting fellow in the Data Semantics (DaSe) Lab.
