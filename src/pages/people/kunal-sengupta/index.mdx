## Bio:

Kunal Sengupta was a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Wright State University, where he worked in the area of knowledge representation and reasoning for the Semantic Web.

After graduation he joined Amazon as a Software Development Engineer.

His experiences include:


- January 2011 – August 2015 – Graduate Research Assistant and PhD student at Wright State University.

- June 2014 – August 2014 – Graduate Research Intern at Siemens Corporate Research.

- June 2013 – August 2013 – Research Intern at fluid Operations, Germany.

- March 2008 – December 2010 – Senior Software Engineer at Saba Software India Pvt Ltd.

- May 2006 – March 2008 – Software Engineer at Infosys Technologies Ltd.

- 2002 – 2006 – Bachelor’s degree (BTech) from the Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology.



## Research Interests:

Semantic Web, Big data, knowledge representation and reasoning, algorithms
