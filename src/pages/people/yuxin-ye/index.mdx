# Yuxin Ye

## Bio:

Yuxin Ye is a visiting scholar at the Data Semantics Laboratory at Wright State University.

She received her PhD degree in 2010 from Jilin University, China.

She joined the College of Computer Science and Technology of Jilin University as a faculty member in the same year and is now an associate professor.

## Research Interests:


- Ontology debugging

- Relation extraction

- Automated population
