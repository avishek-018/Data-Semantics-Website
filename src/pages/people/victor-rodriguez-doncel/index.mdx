## Bio:

Past member of DaSe Lab.

Dr. Víctor Rodríguez‑Doncel (born 1978) is a researcher in the Ontology Engineering Group at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, Spain.

He received his PhD in 2010 from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and has conducted research at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and Universitat Pompeu Fabra as well as industry positions at GMV and Barcelona Digital.

He is editor of the ISO/IEC21000‑19 and ISO/IEC21000‑20 international standards and of the Media Value Chain Ontology and Media Contract Ontology for representing intellectual property along the multimedia value chain and in media contracts.

He holds one granted patent, has written two books and ten JCR‑indexed journal papers, and has participated in FP6 and FP7 framework projects such as VISNET‑I, AXMEDIS, VISNET‑II, SUPERHUB and LIDER.

He visited the Data Semantics Lab at WSU in April/May 2015 and again in August/September 2017 within the Semdata project.
