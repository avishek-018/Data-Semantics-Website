## Bio:

Ilias Tachmazidis is a PhD candidate at the University of Huddersfield, UK.

He investigates whether and how large‑scale reasoning can be achieved through mass parallelization.

His PhD is supervised by Prof. Grigoris Antoniou and he is a member of the PARK (Planning, Autonomy and Representation of Knowledge) research group.

He received a best paper award at RuleML 2013 and gave an invited talk at AAAI‑13.

Ilias holds MSc and BSc degrees from the University of Crete (2012 and 2010).

## Research Interests:


- Knowledge representation and reasoning

- Semantic web

- High‑performance computing

- Distributed computing
