## Bio:

Raghava Mutharaju is a PhD student in the Computer Science department of Wright State University.

His advisor is Prof. Pascal Hitzler and he works closely with Dr. Prabhaker Mateti.

His work focuses on distributed approaches for OWL reasoning algorithms and distributed SPARQL query processing over RDF data.

More details are available on his homepage.

Email: mutharaju.2@wright.edu. Profiles: Google Scholar, LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter.

## Research Interests:

Distributed OWL reasoning, scalable SPARQL query processing, knowledge representation, semantic web applications
