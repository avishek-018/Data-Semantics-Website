## Bio:

Maryam Labaf is a computational science PhD student at the University of Massachusetts in Boston.

Her research interests are mostly in data analysis, machine learning, neural networks and stochastic models of gene expression.

She holds bachelor and master degrees in applied mathematics.

During her master she worked on “Propositional Rule Extraction from Neural Networks Under Background Knowledge” under the supervision of Prof. Pascal Hitzler and Prof. Anthony B. Evans.

She is currently working on stochastic modelling of the regulation of gene expression by microRNAs under the supervision of Prof. Rahul Kulkarni and Kourosh Zarringhalam.
