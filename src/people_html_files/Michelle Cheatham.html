Bio:

Michelle Cheatham spent a decade working for various companies and government organizations before pursuing a PhD.

She graduated in the summer of 2014 and joined the faculty at Wright State University a month later.

Her research interests include semantic data integration, automated population and structuring of knowledge bases, and privacy concerns related to Big Data.
