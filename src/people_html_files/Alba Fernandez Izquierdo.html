Bio:

Alba Fernández Izquierdo is a first-year PhD student in the Ontology Engineering Group at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, Spain.

She received her university degree in Computer Engineering from Universidad de Santiago de Compostela in 2015 and her MSc in Artificial Intelligence from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid in 2016.

She is currently working on ontology testing through ontological requirements and focusing on ontology quality and collaborative engineering.

Research Interests:

Semantic Web, ontology quality, ontology modeling
