Bio:

Lu Zhou is a machine learning engineer and researcher specializing in knowledge graphs and semantic technologies.

In February 2022 he joined TigerGraph, Inc. as a Machine Learning Engineer, working on modelling knowledge graphs and prototyping graph-based machine learning models.

In March 2023 he joined Gloco.ai and in May 2025 joined Apple as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer.

Previously he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Data Semantics Laboratory at Kansas State University, working with Dr. Pascal Hitzler on knowledge graph construction and enrichment, semantic data integration, deep deductive reasoning, ontology matching and alignment, entity linking, coreference resolution, NLP, applied machine learning and neural-symbolic integration.

He can be reached at kbzhoulu@gmail.com.
