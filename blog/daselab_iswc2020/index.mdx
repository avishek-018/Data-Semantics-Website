DaSe Lab members [Pascal Hitzler](/people/pascal-hitzler), [Cogan Shimizu](/people/cogan-shimizu), [Md Kamruzzaman Sarker](/people/kamruzzaman-sarker),  [Lu Zhou](/people/lu-zhou), [Monireh Ebrahimi](/people/monireh-ebrahimi), [Aaron Eberhart](/people/aaron-eberhart), and [Abhilekha Dalal](/people/abhilekha-dalal) attended the [ISWC 2020 Virtual Conference](https://iswc2020.semanticweb.org/) this week. ISWC is a key conference for research in the [semantic web](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_Web). DaSe Lab was very active at [ISWC](https://iswc2020.semanticweb.org/) in 2020!
