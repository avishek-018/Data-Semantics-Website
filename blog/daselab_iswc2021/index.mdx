DaSe Lab members [Pascal Hitzler](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/Pascal-Hitzler), [Cogan Shimizu](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/cogan-shimizu), [Md kamruzzaman Sarker](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/Kamruzzaman-Sarker), [Lu Zhou](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/lu-zhou), [Monireh Ebrahimi](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/Monireh-Ebrahimi), [Joseph Zalewski](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/joseph-zalewski), [Andrew Eells](//daselab.cs.ksu.edu/people/andrew-eells), and alumnus [Raghava Mutharaju](http://raghavam.github.io/) attended the [ISWC 2021 Virtual Conference](https://iswc2021.semanticweb.org/) this week. [ISWC](https://iswc2021.semanticweb.org/) is a key conference for research in the [semantic web](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_Web). DaSe Lab was very active at [ISWC](https://iswc2021.semanticweb.org/) in 2021!
